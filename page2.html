Our services

surveillance systems(cctv)

Closed Circuit TV Systems installed for security purposes are normally used to manage the movement of people around the areas to be protected. The videos captured by these cameras are stored either digitally or conventionally. The digitally stored videos can be accessed from a remote location. 

EPABX

Our systems are smallest in size, most economical & full of world-class features. Our INTERCOM systems are suitable to a small housing society to a very large group of buildings society. Our EPABX System series is suitable for mini offices, homes, and small business organizations to medium Corporate, Factories, Hospitals, Colleges and Hotels etc.

Access control systems

SS17 communications provides Access Control Systems, which constitute an integral part of electronic security systems, with a wide range of products according to the operating scenarios provided by the customer and the risk analysis carried out by our engineers for the fulfillment of multiple purposes.
